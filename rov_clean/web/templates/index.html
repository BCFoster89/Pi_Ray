Thruster Layout

        

          
FL (Front-Left)GPIO pin 8 — surge +, sway −, yaw +

          
FR (Front-Right)GPIO pin 12 — surge +, sway +, yaw −

          
RL (Rear-Left)GPIO pin 16 — surge −, sway −, yaw −

          
RR (Rear-Right)GPIO pin 13 — surge −, sway +, yaw +

          
DescendGPIO pins 6 & 20 — vertical thrust down

          
AscendNot installed (placeholder)

        


        
Dead Reckoning (DR) Map

        
Estimates position by integrating gravity-compensated IMU acceleration. Accuracy degrades over time without a position fix.

        

          
Damp (0.80–1.00)Velocity decay per update. Higher = more momentum/glide; lower = stops quickly.

          
Dead (0.00–1.00)Minimum acceleration threshold. Higher = ignores small vibrations and noise.

          
Scale (0.1–5.0)Acceleration sensitivity multiplier. Higher = map moves more per unit of movement.

          
ResetClears position to origin and erases trail history.

        


        
Depth Hold PID

        
PID controller drives the vertical thrusters to hold a target depth.

        

          
Kp (0–5)Proportional gain — how aggressively thrust corrects depth error. Too high causes oscillation.

          
Ki (0–2)Integral gain — corrects slow persistent drift. Too high causes slow oscillation.

          
Kd (0–2)Derivative gain — dampens overshoot and oscillation. Too high amplifies sensor noise.

        


        
Calibration Buttons

        

          
Zero DepthSets the current depth sensor reading as the 0 ft reference.

          
Zero HorizonLevels the artificial horizon — zeroes pitch and roll offsets.

          
Zero IMUResets gyroscope and accelerometer bias (hold ROV still first).

          
Toggle LEDTurns the ROV's LED lighting on or off.

        


        
Camera Focus

        

          
AutoContinuous autofocus — camera adjusts focus automatically.

          
ManualManual lens position via slider. 0 = infinity (far), 10 = closest macro.

          
AF OnceSingle autofocus shot, then locks focus in place.
